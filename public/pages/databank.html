EEG DataSet

The EEG dataset was approved by the Icahn School of Medicine Institutional Review Board. Simultaneous iEEG and scalp EEG were collected from patients using a Natus XLTEK 128 or Natus Quantum amplifier. A total of 102 seizures of mesial temporal lobe onset (80 seizures were of right mesial temporal onset and 22 were of left mesial temporal onset) were analyzed from 19 patients with DRE who had simultaneous iEEG and scalp EEG as part of their surgical evaluation. Of these 102 seizures, 35 were not seen on the scalp EEG and were surface negative. These seizures were either focal aware or subclinical. Of the remaining 67 seizures, 7 had simultaneous scalp and iEEG onset and 60 had a delayed scalp onset. All patients had 19 bilateral scalp electrode contacts for analysis, placed using the standard 10-10 system. Five patients had unilateral studies (24 IEEG contacts) and the remaining 14 patients had bilateral studies (48 IEEG contacts). For each hemisphere, 24 contacts were used with 4 contacts in each of the following areas: amygdala, lateral anterior temporal, hippocampus, lateral mid temporal, medial orbitofrontal and lateral frontal.
